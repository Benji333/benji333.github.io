Adare Manor is a luxurious resort located in County Limerick, Ireland. The manor was originally built in 1832 as a private residence for the 2nd Earl of Dunraven, but it has since been converted into a luxury hotel and resort. The property boasts 104 guest rooms, top-tier dining options, a spa, fitness center, and indoor pool, as well as stunning grounds and gardens that feature a championship golf course, a walled garden, and a river walk. Adare Manor is a renowned destination for luxury travel and offers guests an unforgettable experience of opulence and leisure amid the Irish countryside.
